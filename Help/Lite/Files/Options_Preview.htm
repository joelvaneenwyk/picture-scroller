Preview

On the main Picture Scroller screen, the third button down from the top on the left side is the "Preview" button.  Clicking this button will display for you the options available for previewing the pictures that you've selected.

Preview

After you've clicked "Preview", the right side of the screen will change to show small thumbnails of the pictures that you have added.  To move through the pictures, use the scrollbar on the right side.  To view an enlargement of a picture, left-click on it.  If the picture is too large to fit the screen, it will automatically be adjusted proportionally.  To exit the enlargement, simply click on the picture.
