Adjust Options

The following section explains the various options available for customizing your scrolling experience.  To access these options, click "Adjust Options" on the left side of the main Picture Scroller screen.  The right side, which will show the options, is divided into four main sections, each of which relates to a specific area of customization.  These are:
Interval
Transitions
Miscellaneous
Advanced
