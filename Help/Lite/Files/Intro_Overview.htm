Overview

As many computer users have discovered, it can be quite frustrating sorting through all the pictures he or she may have.  The main purpose of Picture Scroller, therefore, is to remedy this problem by allowing you to scroll through all the pictures in any directory that you have on your hard drive or even on a remote drive.  Besides that, it can scroll through the pictures with 10 different transitions, adding greatly to the picture scrolling experience!  This could be used to give a slide presentation to your company, or just to browse through your pictures.  Picture Scroller makes it easy to view and use any picture that you have acquired from a digital camera, scanner, the Internet, or even those you have drawn yourself!
