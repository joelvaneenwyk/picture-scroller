Enabling the Screen Saver

To enable Picture Scroller as your screen saver, follow these steps:

  1. Show the "Display Properties" window which will allow you to select the screen saver you want to use.  You can do this in one of two ways:

    1. After clicking the "Start" button, move up to "Settings" and click "Control Panel".  When the "Control Panel " appears, locate and double-click the "Display" icon.

    2. After right-clicking on the desktop, click "Properties", which is the last option in the menu that appeared when you right-clicked.

    Either option you choose will bring up the "Display Properties" window.

  2. Click "Screen Saver" at the top of the "Display Properties" window.

  3. To the left of the "Settings" button, there is an option to select the screen saver that you want to use.  Click the arrow to show a list of all the screen savers on your computer.
  4. Move through the list until you come to "Picture Scroller"; click it.
  5. Click "Apply" or "OK" at the bottom to complete the process of enabling Picture Scroller as your screen saver.

Now that Picture Scroller is enabled on your computer, you may want to go to the Setting Screen Saver Options section which explains how to select the pictures to be used, as well as how to adjust the screen saver options.

If at any time you would like to discontinue using Picture Scroller as your screen saver, follow the steps above.  When you come to step 4, instead of selecting "Picture Scroller", select the alternative screen saver that you want to use.
