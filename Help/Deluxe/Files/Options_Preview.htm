Preview and Printing

On the main Picture Scroller screen, the third button down from the top on the left side is the "Preview and Printing" button.  Clicking this button will display for you the options available for previewing or printing the pictures that you've selected.

Preview

After you've clicked "Preview and Printing", the right side of the screen will change to show small thumbnails of the pictures that you have added.  To move through the pictures, use the scrollbar on the right side.  To view an enlargement of a picture, left-click on it.  If the picture is too large to fit the screen, it will automatically be adjusted proportionally.  To exit the enlargement, simply click on the picture.

Printing

Also available in this section are the options for printing your pictures.  Clicking "Print Pictures" will bring up a window with these options.  On the right side is a scaled-down view of the page, while on the left side are buttons for adding and removing pictures as well as for setting up the printer and actually printing the page.

Select Picture(s)

When you click "Select Picture(s)", a list of all the pictures that you've selected will be displayed.  Checking an item from the list will add that picture to the page to be printed.  When you've selected all the pictures you would like to print, click "OK".  Picture Scroller will then load the pictures onto the page. 

Now that the pictures are on the page, you can:

Arrange them by clicking and dragging them around.
Resize a picture by dragging an edge or corner in whichever direction you choose.  To restore a picture to its original size, right-click it and click "Restore".
Remove

Instead of removing a picture by going into "Select Picture(s)", the "Remove" button allows you to instantly remove whichever picture is selected.  Just click on the picture you wish to remove, then click "Remove".

Setup

The "Setup" button allows you to change/adjust printing options such as paper size, layout, etc.

Print

When all your pictures are arrange to your satisfaction, click "Print" to proceed.

Picture Stacking

The "Picture Stacking" slider allows you to bring your pictures forward or backwards (in front of or behind) the other pictures on the page.  To use this, click on the picture whose order you would like to change and drag the slider left or right.  The farther to the left the slider is, the farther back the picture will be.
