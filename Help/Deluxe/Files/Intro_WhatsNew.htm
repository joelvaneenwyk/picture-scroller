What's New Since 1.0

Since the release of Picture Scroller 1.0 on March 25, 2000, Picture Scroller has undergone massive changes.  The new Picture Scroller 2.0 features many additions, as well as changes to the old.  For changes in version 2.1, see below.

  • A Fresh, Intuitive Look

As of this release, Picture Scroller has undergone a massive facelift.  With its step-by-step design, now all you have to do is follow the buttons on the left part of the main screen, and you'll be up and scrolling in no time!  Besides that, Picture Scroller also features a much-improved help system, enabling you to get the most out of Picture Scroller.  Whenever you want to know what something does, just move your mouse over it to see a description at the bottom of the Picture Scroller screen.  If that's not enough, press the F1 key on your keyboard for more information.

  • Support for Multiple Folders

Instead of selecting just one folder at a time, now you can scroll through all the pictures from more than one place simultaneously!  This also includes the ability to save lists of chosen pictures for future viewing.  More Information

  • Set the Exact Order of the Pictures

Why let Picture Scroller determine the viewing order of the pictures for you? In Picture Scroller 2.0, you can decide not only what pictures are shown, but also in what order they will appear.  More Information

  • Preview Pictures

With this feature, you can look over the pictures you've chosen before scrolling through them, helping you to determine the best possible scrolling order.  More Information

  • More Picture Formats

Now, you can scroll through even more different picture formats, including Photoshop and PCX pictures. More Information

  • Even More Transitions

This version of Picture Scroller now comes packed with 50 transitions to choose from, more than double that of the previous version!  Among the most notable are the new circle transitions.  More Information

  • Use as a Screen Saver

Being able to scroll through your vacation photos is one thing, but how about having your computer automatically scroll through those pictures every time you're away from your computer, so that when you come back, you see one of those special memories sliding gently across the screen?  That is pure satisfaction!  Now with the Picture Scroller 2.0 Screen Saver feature, you can!  More Information

  • Advanced Options

With the addition of an advanced options section, you can choose the color that backdrops your pictures and adjust the screen resolution used while scrolling through the pictures.  More Information

  • Confirmed Support for Windows 95, 98, and 2000

Picture Scroller 2.0 has now been verified to work on Windows 95, 98, 2000 and XP.  More Information

What's New In 2.1

Picture Scroller 2.1 fixes various graphical "glitches" that appear when using Picture Scroller on Windows 2000 or XP.  In particular, when scrolling through pictures in "Proportional" and "Screen Size" mode, pictures no longer have black lines or dots scattered across each picture.  Also, pictures appear very crisp when viewing their preview, both when selecting pictures and in Print Preview.
