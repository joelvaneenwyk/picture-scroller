Setting Options

The next step to scrolling through your pictures is setting the time interval between pictures.  To do this, just click "Adjust Options" on the left side of the main Picture Scroller screen.   This time, the right half of the screen will change to show "tabs" on the top with various options below.  The first "Interval" tab has a dial allowing you to adjust the interval time.  Clicking anywhere on the dial will lengthen or shorten the interval time.  By default, the delay is set to three seconds, but you can change this is to anywhere from one second to twenty seconds.
