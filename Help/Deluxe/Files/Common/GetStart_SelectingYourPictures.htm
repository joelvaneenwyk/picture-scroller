Selecting Your Pictures

The first step to scrolling through your pictures is choosing the pictures you would like to use.  To do this, follow these steps:

  1. Click the "Select Pictures" button on the left side of the main Picture Scroller screen.  The right side of the screen now changes to show a series of buttons along the top with a blank area below.
  2. Click the "Add" button, which is the first in the series of buttons.  A list menu will appear with choices including "Directory" and  "File(s)".

  3. At this point, if you would like to add an entire directory (folder) of pictures, click "Directory".  Or if you would like to add individual files, click "File(s)".  Either way, just select the directory or files that you want to use (Tip); then, click "OK".
