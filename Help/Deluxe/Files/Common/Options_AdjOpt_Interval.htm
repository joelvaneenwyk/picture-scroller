Adjust Options - Interval

The first section of options is the Interval section.  Here you can change the amount of time between the pictures as they are scrolled through.  The dial on the right, as well as the white box on its left, shows the interval time currently being used.  By default, this is three seconds.  This can be changed to anywhere from a rapid one-second interval to a leisurely twenty seconds.

There are two ways to adjust the time:

  1. Click on the dial and drag your mouse around to adjust the time, or
  2. Change the number in the white box to the left of the dial.
