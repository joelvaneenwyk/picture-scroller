While Scrolling

After you've pressed the "Begin" button, and as you're scrolling through your pictures, other useful options are available for your scrolling enjoyment.  Each of these options can be implemented by pressing the corresponding key on your keyboard.  Below is a table of the various option keys:

H

 		This is the most important key of all.  Pressing this will display a series of buttons along the bottom of your screen, and will also "un-hide" the mouse.*  This will make it possible for you to move forward or backwards through the pictures or exit scrolling to return to the main menu. 

Left & Right Keys

 These keys also allow you to move forward or backwards through the pictures. **
Space Bar Pressing this will pause the scrolling of the pictures.  Press it again to resume scrolling.
F1 Press this to show a list of all these keys.
F2 Shows information near the bottom of the screen about the picture currently shown. 
F3 Shows options allowing you to make the current picture your background (wallpaper).
Escape (ESC) Immediately stops the scrolling of pictures and returns you to the main menu.

* While you are scrolling through pictures, your mouse is deliberately hidden so as not to hinder the scrolling experience.  However, if at any time you would like to see the mouse, just press the "H" key on your keyboard (see above).

** The left and right mouse buttons can also be used to move backwards or forward (respectively) through the pictures.
